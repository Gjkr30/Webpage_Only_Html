Intoduction

This is not the story about a boy who wants to be a Hokage. It is a new story about the person who dreams to achive something big. Being the son of a Hokage it is expected by him to carry out the legacy left by his ancestors. We are going to witness a exciting journey of the Shinobi world from the eyes of Boruto Uzumaki. This arc basically revolves around the invasion of Otutsuki Clan to Earth.
